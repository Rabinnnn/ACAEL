How Recognized is the Degree Obtained?

ACAEL works with institutions / universities that must be government accredited in their own countries and must also be listed in the UNESCO Handbook of World Universities.

This ensures that the degree you achieve is recognized, by convention, in any country hence accepted worldwide.

As a testimonial, and ahead of your registration for any programme under our Experiential Accreditation, we will provide you with evidence of government recognition of degrees of past graduates.
